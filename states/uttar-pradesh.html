Uttar Pradesh

Uttar Pradesh, often called the “Heartland of India,” is the most populous state in the country. Known for its historical monuments, spiritual cities, and rich Mughal heritage, it is home to the iconic Taj Mahal, the sacred Ganges River, and vibrant festivals. Cities like Varanasi, Lucknow, and Agra make it a cultural treasure.

Capital: Lucknow
Pop: ~240M

Fun Facts
  • - Home to three UNESCO World Heritage Sites.
  • - Varanasi is one of the oldest living cities in the world.
  • - Major producer of sugarcane and wheat.
  • - Kumbh Mela here holds the record for the largest gathering of people.
