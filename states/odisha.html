Odisha

Odisha, known for its ancient temples, vibrant festivals, and long coastline along the Bay of Bengal, is a land of spiritual and natural beauty. From the Sun Temple at Konark to the Rath Yatra of Puri, Odisha blends heritage, art, and scenic wonders.

Capital: Bhubaneswar
Pop: ~46M
Puri Beach
Konark Temple
Fun Facts
  • - The Jagannath Temple in Puri is one of the Char Dham pilgrimage sites.
  • - Chilika Lake is Asia’s largest brackish water lagoon.
  • - Known for Odissi, one of India’s oldest classical dance forms.
  • - Famous for appliqué work from Pipili.
