Sikkim

Sikkim, nestled in the Eastern Himalayas, is India’s smallest and one of the most scenic states. Known for its snow-capped mountains, Buddhist monasteries, and lush green valleys, it’s a paradise for nature and adventure lovers. The mighty Kanchenjunga, the third-highest peak in the world, dominates its skyline.

Capital: Gangtok
Pop: ~0.7M

Fun Facts

  • - Known for vibrant festivals like Losar and Saga Dawa.
