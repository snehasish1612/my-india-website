Dadra and Nagar Haveli and Daman and Diu

A union territory formed by merging two distinct regions, this destination combines lush forests, serene beaches, and Portuguese colonial charm. Dadra and Nagar Haveli lies inland with tribal heritage, while Daman and Diu are coastal gems dotted with forts, churches, and golden sands.

Fun Facts
  • - Officially merged into one UT in January 2020.
  • - Daman and Diu were under Portuguese rule for over 400 years.
  • - Dadra and Nagar Haveli has over 60% forest cover.
