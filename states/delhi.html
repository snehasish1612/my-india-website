Delhi

Delhi, India’s bustling capital territory, is a vibrant mix of ancient history and modern urban life. From the Mughal-era Red Fort to the Parliament House, it serves as the nation’s political heart while offering a treasure trove of cultural and culinary experiences.

Fun Facts

  • - Home to Qutub Minar, the tallest brick minaret in the world.
  • - Has one of the largest metro networks globally.
  • - Chandni Chowk is among the oldest and busiest markets in India.
