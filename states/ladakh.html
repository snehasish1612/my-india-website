Ladakh

Ladakh, known as the "Land of High Passes," is a rugged region in northern India, famous for its breathtaking landscapes, Buddhist monasteries, and crystal-clear lakes. Its unique culture reflects Tibetan traditions, and the region attracts travelers seeking adventure, spirituality, and peace.
