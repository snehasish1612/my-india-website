Assam

Assam, the gateway to Northeast India, is famous for its tea gardens, rich biodiversity, and the mighty Brahmaputra River. It is home to the UNESCO World Heritage Site Kaziranga National Park, known for the one-horned rhinoceros, and the vibrant Bihu festival. Assam’s culture blends indigenous traditions, classical arts, and natural beauty.
