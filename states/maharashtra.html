Maharashtra

Maharashtra, India’s third-largest state, is known for its cosmopolitan capital Mumbai, historic forts, UNESCO heritage caves, and scenic Western Ghats. It blends Bollywood glamour, vibrant festivals, and deep-rooted Maratha history.

Capital: Mumbai
Pop: ~124M
Bollywood
Western Ghats
Fun Facts
  • - Gateway of India is one of its most famous landmarks.
  • - Ajanta & Ellora Caves are UNESCO World Heritage Sites.
  • - Home to India’s largest film industry.
  • - Known for forts of Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj.
