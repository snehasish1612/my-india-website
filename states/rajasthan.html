Rajasthan

Rajasthan, the “Land of Kings,” is India’s largest state and a treasure trove of history, culture, and architecture. Famous for its majestic forts, vibrant festivals, and the Thar Desert, it offers a royal experience steeped in tradition. The cities of Jaipur, Udaipur, and Jaisalmer are jewels in its crown.

Capital: Jaipur
Pop: ~80M
Forts & Palaces
Thar Desert
Fun Facts
  • - Home to six UNESCO World Heritage forts.
  • - Hosts the famous Pushkar Camel Fair annually.
  • - Udaipur is known as the “City of Lakes.”
  • - Houses the world’s largest stone sundial at Jantar Mantar, Jaipur.
