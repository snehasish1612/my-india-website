Himachal Pradesh

Himachal Pradesh, nestled in the lap of the Himalayas, is a paradise for nature lovers and adventure seekers. Famous for its apple orchards, hill stations like Shimla and Manali, and serene monasteries, the state offers breathtaking views and a peaceful escape from the bustle.

Capital: Shimla*
Pop: ~7.4M
Himalayan Ranges
Apple Orchards
Fun Facts
  • - Dharamshala is home to the Dalai Lama and Tibetan government-in-exile.
  • - Known as the “Apple State” of India for its large production.
  • - Has some of India’s highest cricket stadiums and ski slopes.
  • - Summer capital (Shimla) and winter capital (Dharamshala).
*Shimla (summer) & Dharamshala (winter) share capital status.
