Madhya Pradesh

Madhya Pradesh, the “Heart of India,” is known for its rich cultural heritage, magnificent forts, ancient temples, and stunning natural reserves. From the Khajuraho temples to the dense jungles of Kanha and Bandhavgarh, it offers both history and wildlife adventures.

Capital: Bhopal
Pop: ~86M
Tiger Reserves
Khajuraho
Fun Facts
  • - Has the largest number of tigers among all Indian states.
  • - Khajuraho temples are a UNESCO World Heritage Site.
  • - Known for Bhimbetka rock shelters with prehistoric paintings.
  • - Famous for Sanchi Stupa, an important Buddhist monument.
