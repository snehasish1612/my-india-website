Bihar

Bihar, one of the oldest inhabited regions in the world, is steeped in history, culture, and spirituality. It is the birthplace of Buddhism and Jainism, and home to ancient sites like Nalanda University and Bodh Gaya. Rich in agriculture, festivals, and folk traditions, Bihar is a vibrant mix of heritage and resilience.
