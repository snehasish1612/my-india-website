Karnataka

Karnataka is a land where ancient history meets cutting-edge technology. From the IT hub of Bengaluru to the heritage sites of Hampi and the lush coffee plantations of Coorg, the state is rich in culture, nature, and innovation.

Capital: Bengaluru
Pop: ~68M
IT Hub
Coffee Plantations
Fun Facts
  • - Hampi is a UNESCO World Heritage Site with stunning ruins.
  • - Karnataka is the largest coffee producer in India.
  • - Home to Mysore Palace, a marvel of Indo-Saracenic architecture.
  • - Known for its classical Carnatic music tradition.
