Jharkhand

Jharkhand, the “Land of Forests,” is rich in minerals, waterfalls, and tribal culture. Known for industrial hubs like Jamshedpur and Bokaro, as well as scenic sites like Netarhat and Betla National Park, the state is a unique blend of economic power and natural charm.

Capital: Ranchi
Pop: ~39M

Fun Facts
  • - Produces over 40% of India's mineral wealth.
  • - Jamshedpur is India’s first planned industrial city.
  • - Betla National Park is home to tigers and elephants.
  • - Ranchi is known for its pleasant weather and waterfalls.
