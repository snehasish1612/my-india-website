West Bengal

West Bengal, stretching from the Himalayas in the north to the Bay of Bengal in the south, is a land of rich culture, literature, and natural beauty. Known for its colonial heritage, the bustling city of Kolkata, the Sundarbans mangrove forests, and tea gardens of Darjeeling, the state blends tradition with modern vibrance.

Capital: Kolkata
Pop: ~100M

Fun Facts
  • - Kolkata is called the "Cultural Capital of India".
  • - Sundarbans is home to the Royal Bengal Tiger.
  • - Famous for Durga Puja, a UNESCO intangible heritage festival.
  • - Darjeeling Himalayan Railway is a UNESCO World Heritage site.
