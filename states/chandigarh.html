Chandigarh

Chandigarh, the shared capital of Punjab and Haryana, is India’s first planned city, designed by the legendary architect Le Corbusier. Known for its clean roads, green gardens, and modernist architecture, it represents order, beauty, and urban innovation.

Fun Facts
  • - Designed by Le Corbusier, a world-famous Swiss-French architect.
  • - Home to the famous Nek Chand’s Rock Garden, built from waste materials.
  • - Has one of the highest per capita incomes in India.
  • - Known for its grid-like sectors and wide tree-lined roads.
