Jammu & Kashmir

Jammu & Kashmir, often called "Paradise on Earth," is known for its snow-capped mountains, serene lakes, lush valleys, and rich cultural heritage. From the Mughal gardens of Srinagar to the Vaishno Devi shrine in Jammu, this union territory offers breathtaking natural beauty and deep spiritual significance.
