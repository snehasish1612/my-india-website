Goa

Goa, India’s sunshine state, is famed for its golden beaches, Portuguese heritage, and vibrant nightlife. With palm-fringed shores, spice plantations, and colorful festivals, it’s a fusion of East and West that attracts travelers from all over the globe.

Capital: Panaji
Pop: ~1.6M
Beaches
Carnival
Fun Facts
  • - Portuguese ruled Goa for over 450 years.
  • - Famous for Feni, a local cashew-based liquor.
  • - Basilica of Bom Jesus is a UNESCO World Heritage Site.
  • - Known for Asia’s largest electronic music festival — Sunburn.
