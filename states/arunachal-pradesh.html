Arunachal Pradesh

Arunachal Pradesh, known as the “Land of the Rising Sun” in India, is a northeastern state blessed with breathtaking mountains, lush valleys, and rich tribal heritage. It shares borders with Bhutan, China, and Myanmar, making it a place of unique cultural fusion. The state is famed for its monasteries, wildlife, and adventure tourism.

Capital: Itanagar
Pop: ~1.6M

Fun Facts
  • - Known as the “Orchid State of India” with over 500 orchid species.
  • - Home to Tawang Monastery, the largest in India.
  • - Over 26 major tribes and 100+ sub-tribes live here.
  • - Famous for Ziro Music Festival attracting artists worldwide.
