Tamil Nadu

Tamil Nadu, located in the southernmost part of India, is celebrated for its ancient Dravidian-style temples, classical Bharatanatyam dance, and a rich cultural heritage dating back over 2,000 years. From the bustling city of Chennai to the serene Nilgiri Hills, it’s a land where tradition meets modern industry.

Capital: Chennai
Pop: ~77M

Fun Facts
  • - Has the most UNESCO World Heritage sites in India (temples of Mahabalipuram, etc.).
  • - The Nilgiri Mountain Railway is a UNESCO World Heritage site.
  • - Major producer of silk and cotton textiles.
  • - Birthplace of the Tamil language, one of the world’s oldest living languages.
