Andaman & Nicobar Islands

The Andaman & Nicobar Islands, an archipelago of over 500 islands in the Bay of Bengal, are famous for their pristine beaches, coral reefs, tropical forests, and unique tribal cultures. Once a key colonial penal settlement, today they are a paradise for nature lovers and divers.

Capital: Port Blair

Fun Facts
  • - Home to the famous Cellular Jail, a historical freedom struggle landmark.
  • - Radhanagar Beach is ranked among the best beaches in Asia.
  • - Some islands are restricted to protect indigenous tribes.
  • - A paradise for snorkelling, diving, and birdwatching.
