Uttarakhand

Uttarakhand, often called the “Land of the Gods” (Devbhumi), is a Himalayan state known for its breathtaking landscapes, pilgrimage sites, and adventure tourism. From the holy Char Dham shrines to snow-capped peaks and serene lakes, it’s a paradise for spiritual seekers and nature lovers alike.

Capitals: Dehradun & Gairsain
Pop: ~11M
Himalayan Peaks
Char Dham
Fun Facts
  • - Source of the Ganga and Yamuna rivers.
  • - Jim Corbett National Park is India’s first national park.
  • - Known for the Valley of Flowers UNESCO site.
  • - Popular for trekking, river rafting, and yoga retreats.
