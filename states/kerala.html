Kerala

Kerala, often called “God’s Own Country,” is a tropical paradise with serene backwaters, lush greenery, spice plantations, and stunning beaches. Known for Ayurveda, Kathakali dance, and a high literacy rate, it is a blend of nature, tradition, and modern living.

Capital: Thiruvananthapuram
Pop: ~35M
Backwaters
Ayurveda
Fun Facts
  • - Has the highest literacy rate among Indian states (~96%).
  • - Known for boat races during Onam festival.
  • - A top producer of spices like pepper and cardamom.
  • - Houses the famous Periyar Wildlife Sanctuary.
