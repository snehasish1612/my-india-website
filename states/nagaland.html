Nagaland

Nagaland, the “Land of Festivals,” is nestled in the hills of Northeast India. Known for its vibrant tribal heritage, the world-famous Hornbill Festival, and breathtaking landscapes, the state is a mosaic of cultures and scenic beauty.

Capital: Kohima
Pop: ~2.3M

Fun Facts
  • - Known as the “Falcon Capital of the World” due to Amur falcon migration.
  • - Home to 16 major tribes, each with unique traditions.
  • - Dzukou Valley is called the “Valley of Flowers of the Northeast.”
  • - Rich culture of folk music and warrior dances.
