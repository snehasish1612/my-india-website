Punjab

Punjab, the “Land of Five Rivers,” is known for its fertile fields, rich traditions, and vibrant festivals. It is the birthplace of Sikhism and home to the iconic Golden Temple in Amritsar. The state is famous for its lively bhangra dance, hearty cuisine, and warm hospitality.

Capital: Chandigarh
Pop: ~30M
Golden Temple
Agricultural Hub
Fun Facts
  • - Amritsar’s Golden Temple serves free meals to over 50,000 people daily.
  • - Known as India’s breadbasket due to its high wheat production.
  • - Bhangra and Giddha are popular folk dances originating here.
  • - The Wagah Border ceremony attracts thousands every day.
